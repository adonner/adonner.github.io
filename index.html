Hello, I'm Adam Donner


"I want to put a ding in the universe." - Steve Jobs

Adam Donner

Web Developer, Marketing Professional, Product Manager

I have spent the past 15 years of my career in the software industry in both the engineering and sales/marketing divisions.

I received my first degree from the University of Nebraska with a Bachelors in Management with an Information Systems focus.

After graduation I started my career as a oftware engineer for a publically traded company that develops hardware devices.

I spent four years as an software engineer for that organization before moving into a Regional Manager of both the engineers and software salespeople.

I later accepted a position as a Regional Sales Manager at a software company that develops voice and imaging technology. As a Regional Sales Manager I was responsible for a quarter of the US in my vertical market. I was traveling across the country for work every week until my twin boys, Owen and Alexander were born.

After five years I decided to it was time get off the road. I accepted the position of Director of Sales and Marketing for a local company where my team and I were responsible for selling and developing e-commerce sites for key accounts in Nebraska and Iowa.

I am currently a student at Bellevue University studying Web Development.

I live in Omaha Nebraska with my beautiful wife, twin boys, and golden retriever. In my spare time, I enjoy camping, mountain biking, and hiking.
